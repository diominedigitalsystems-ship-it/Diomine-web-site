About Diomine Digital Systems

À propos de Diomine Digital Systems

Diomine Digital Systems is a young African leading innovator in mining software engineering, specializing in solutions for geological auditing, drillhole planning, mapping, and structural analysis. Our mission is to empower mining professionals with cutting-edge tools that transform complex field data into actionable insights, streamline workflows, and enhance decision-making efficiency.

Diomine Digital Systems est un jeune acteur africain majeur dans l'ingénierie logicielle minière, spécialisé dans les solutions d'audit géologique, de planification des forages, de cartographie et d'analyse structurale. Notre mission est de fournir aux professionnels de la mine des outils innovants qui transforment des données complexes sur le terrain en informations exploitables, simplifient les flux de travail et améliorent la prise de décision.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between field data collection and advanced geospatial modeling, Diomine Digital Systems combines expertise in software development, geosciences, and data analytics to deliver reliable, intuitive, and high-performance solutions. Our flagship products, including Diomine DH Auditor and Diomine MRN, are trusted by geologists and mining engineers worldwide to optimize exploration, grade control, and operational planning.

Fondée avec pour objectif de combler le fossé entre la collecte de données sur le terrain et la modélisation géospatiale avancée, Diomine Digital Systems combine l'expertise en développement logiciel, en géosciences et en analyse de données pour offrir des solutions fiables, intuitives et performantes. Nos produits phares, notamment Diomine DH Auditor et Diomine MRN, sont utilisés par des géologues et ingénieurs miniers à travers le monde pour optimiser l'exploration, le contrôle de minerai et la planification opérationnelle.

At Diomine, we believe that precision, innovation, and user-centered design are the keys to transforming mining challenges into simple, manageable processes. With our software, "Mining becomes an easy game."

Chez Diomine, nous croyons que la précision, l'innovation et la conception centrée sur l'utilisateur sont les clés pour transformer les défis miniers en processus simples et maîtrisables. Avec nos logiciels, "l'exploitation minière devient un jeu facile."

DH Auditor 2026.1.2

Structural mapping and stereographic analysis

This video showcases the advanced features of Diomine DH Auditor 2026, a tool designed to streamline the work of structural geologists and mining engineers through simplified stereographic analysis. Unlike other software that requires creating separate tables for each structure type, DH Auditor allows for the import of a global database (Excel or CSV) and uses dynamic filters to instantly isolate elements such as quartz veins or schistosity. The tutorial details visual plot customization, the choice between Schmidt projections (for statistics) and Wulff projections (for angles), as well as 3D data visualization. Finally, the author demonstrates how to export results, including an automatic calculation of Mean Plans (Main Plan), which is essential for precise future drill planning.

DH Auditor v2026.1.2

Auditing field photographs and georeferenced texture inspection

In the competitive fields of geological exploration and mining, field data precision is the cornerstone of success, and the latest Diomine DH Auditor (v2026.1.2) introduces a powerful feature designed to bridge the gap between field photography and 3D modeling by transforming outcrop photos into actionable geospatial objects. By allowing geologists to precisely locate outcrops at their exact geographic position, attach direct geological descriptions to high-resolution images, and streamline audits through simplified data verification, this update ensures that field photographs are no longer static, disconnected files. The process, demonstrated by expert Diander Newton, is engineered for speed and intuition: users simply navigate to the "Import Georeferenced Image" tool, enter the precise X, Y, and Z coordinates, and enrich the data with technical metadata such as vein orientation or alteration zones. Beyond simple data management and grouping, the software enhances spatial interaction through an interactive pop-up mode for detailed texture inspection and Google Maps integration for real-world topographic cross-referencing.

DH Auditor v2026.1.2

Optimizing exploration and grade control drillhole planning

In this technical demonstration, expert Newton Diomandé showcases the power and flexibility of Diomine DH Auditor (v2026.1.2) for the precise planning of drilling programs and soil geochemistry. The software stands out with its unique import channel capable of handling various formats (CSV, DXF, GPX) and its seamless integration with Google satellite basemaps for real-time geographic positioning. Thanks to the intuitive "Generate Drilling" tool, users can instantly configure point grids with automatic ID incrementing, define the orientation of survey lines (layons), and adjust drill spacing with millimeter precision. Management is further streamlined by a dynamic attribute table that allows for direct data export to Excel, GPX, or DXF, ensuring a fast and efficient workflow for both exploration and mine geologists.
