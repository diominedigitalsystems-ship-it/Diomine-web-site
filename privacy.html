We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily submit it (for example, through our contact form or by emailing us). Our website may collect non-personal information automatically, including browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you may opt out of third-party cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve it.
  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences such as language settings.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our website.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as GitHub, PayPal, Ko-fi, YouTube, and Google Maps. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read their respective privacy policies.

Diomine Digital Systems software products (Diomine DH Auditor, Diomine MRN) operate entirely on your local machine. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any data you process using our software. Your data stays in your own working environment. The only circumstance in which we would see your data is if you choose to send it to us yourself.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our website. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
